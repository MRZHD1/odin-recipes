The World's Best Cookie Recipe

Description

The world's best cookie dough is a wonderfully crunchy cookie that makes a lot and keeps for a long time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 0.5 cups flaked coconut
  • 1 cup crushed cornflakes cereal
  • 0.5 cups chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (170 degrees C)
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Mix in egg, oil, vanilla, baking soda, and flour until well blended.
  4. Stir in oats, coconut, cornflakes, and pecans until incorporated. Drop spoonfuls onto cookie sheets. If desired, sprinkle with white sugar before baking.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned and edges are set, 12 to 15 minutes.
